Carpenters for Housing Reform Victoria

Industry-led initiative exploring a renovation-focused pathway for experienced carpenters — while builders remain responsible for new homes, new-home framing, extensions, and major works.

Clear boundary

This pathway is renovation-focused and is not intended to cover new-home framing or new home construction.

What carpenters could lead (renovation-focused)

  • Bathrooms, laundries, kitchens (renovation coordination)
  • Window and door upgrades / replacement
  • Internal fit-off carpentry (doors, trim, hardware, finish carpentry)
  • Decks / pergolas / verandas (where applicable)
  • Small granny flats / studios up to 30 m² (single storey)
