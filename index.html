Why this matters

Most expensive property mistakes start with a bad assumption, not a dramatic event.

Reno Master AI exists for the moment before money is locked in. It helps you slow down, understand what could go wrong, and judge whether the numbers, scope, and renovation logic actually stack up.

Buying risk

Hidden work after settlement

What looks cosmetic can hide waterproofing failure, structural movement, poor sequencing, or a much bigger rectification scope than expected.

Common outcome: an extra $15,000–$40,000 you did not plan for.
Renovation risk

Scope drift and budget blowouts

A weak plan creates cascading cost. Layout changes, trade coordination, and missed prerequisites can quickly turn a manageable project into a stressful one.

Common outcome: budget confidence collapses before the job is even stable.
Selling risk

Money spent in the wrong place

Sellers often invest in updates that do not materially improve trust, buyer confidence, or sale value because the real blockers were never diagnosed properly.

Common outcome: wasted spend plus weaker buyer confidence.
