About RRC

Our Mission

The Rental Regulations Committee (RRC) is dedicated to ensuring fair rental practices and advocating for the rights of tenants in our community. We work to promote transparency, fairness, and accountability in the rental housing market.

Our Values

  • Fairness: Equal treatment for all parties
  • Transparency: Open communication and honesty
  • Accountability: Responsibility in all actions
  • Community: Serving our neighborhoods
